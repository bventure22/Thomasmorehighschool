Life at Machrina

Our Patron & Values

Inspired by the life and unwavering ethics of St. Thomas More, Machrina High School is deeply dedicated to cultivating integrity, relentless hard work, and profound spiritual growth in every student.

"I die the King's good servant, but God's first."
- St. Thomas More

We believe education is more than mere academics; it is the comprehensive formation of the whole person. Our beautiful campus serves as a bridge to university life, emphasizing independence alongside moral responsibility.
