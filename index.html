Our Patron & Values

Inspired by the life of St. Thomas More, Machrina High School is dedicated to cultivating integrity, hard work, and spiritual growth in every student.

"I die the King's good servant, but God's first."
- St. Thomas More

We believe education is more than academics; it is the formation of the whole person. Our campus serves as a bridge to university life, emphasizing independence alongside moral responsibility.
